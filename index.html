SRM University

VIT was established with the aim of providing quality higher education on par with international standards. It persistently seeks and adopts innovative methods to improve the quality of higher education on a consistent basis.The campus has a cosmopolitan atmosphere with students from all corners of the globe. Manipal University

Manipal University, located in Manipal, Karnataka, India, is a prestigious private research and deemed university established in 1953 by Dr. T. M. A. Pai. Acquiring deemed university status in 1993, it offers a diverse array of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs spanning disciplines such as engineering, medicine, business, arts, and sciences. The university has forged international collaborations, fostering a global academic atmosphere. Notably, Kasturba Medical College (KMC) within Manipal University is renowned for medical and health sciences, while Manipal Institute of Technology (MIT) excels in engineering and technology. The institution prioritizes research and innovation, boasting dedicated centers for cutting-edge exploration. The campus is equipped with modern facilities, including laboratories, libraries, sports complexes, and hostels. Emphasizing holistic development, Manipal University encourages students to engage in cultural, extracurricular, and sports activities, contributing to a well-rounded educational experience.
