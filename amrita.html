Amrita University

About the College

Views: 0

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, or Amrita University, is a privately recognized university located in Coimbatore, India. It holds a prestigious NAAC A++ accreditation and has been ranked as the 7th best university in India according to the NIRF Rankings of 2023. This multi-campus institution encompasses a wide range of disciplines and consists of 15 schools distributed across six campuses in southern India. Currently, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham offers a total of 207 programs, including undergraduate, postgraduate, doctoral, integrated, dual-degree, and certification courses in fields like Engineering, Management, Medical and Life Sciences, Arts and Humanities, and Social Sciences.

Among the courses offered, BTech, MTech, and MBA are particularly popular at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham. Admission to the BTech program requires candidates to participate in either the JEE Main or AEEE entrance examinations. MTech admissions are granted based on GATE/PGCET/TANCET/PGECET scores, while MBA admissions are contingent on CAT/XAT/MAT/CMAT/GMAT/GRE scores. For the latest updates on admissions for the year 2023, prospective students are encouraged to visit the university’s official website.

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is known for its robust placement opportunities, with over 90% of its students securing positions in renowned companies each year. The highest salary package offered to graduates stands at INR 43.30 lakhs per annum, while the average package is INR 5.40 lakhs per annum. Some of the prominent recruiters include well-known companies like Amazon, Wipro, Dell, Samsung, Tech Mahindra, TCS, Infosys, Adani, and many others.

Admission

Admissions to Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham for the year 2023 primarily hinge on the results of entrance exams. For the flagship BTech program, students are admitted based on their scores in either the JEE Main or the Amrita Engineering Entrance Exam (AEEE). In addition to BTech, AVV provides a diverse range of programs, including BA, MTech, MBA, M.Sc, M.A, MSW, and Ph.D. These various courses open up opportunities for students across different academic disciplines and levels of study.


Placements

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham has successfully concluded its placement drive for the MBA batch of 2022, and it has been nothing short of remarkable. With more than 200 companies participating, the outcome was exceptionally positive, as 95% of the students secured placement opportunities. This achievement underscores the university's robust industry connections and the exceptional quality of education and preparation provided to its students. The pinnacle of this success was the highest salary package offered, an impressive INR 56.95 lakhs per annum, demonstrating the value of an education at this institution.

The preceding year, 2021, also witnessed a strong placement season at Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, with a diverse array of over 200 recruiters converging on the campus. Esteemed companies such as Amazon, Microsoft, and Intel were among the major participants, representing various sectors including manufacturing, business analytics, finance consulting, IT, and marketing. Notably, the Product Development sector attracted the highest number of recruiters. What's particularly remarkable is that 100% of the MBA batch secured placements during this drive, underscoring the university's unwavering commitment to advancing the career growth of its students.

In the 2021 placement season, the highest salary package reached INR 56.95 lakhs per annum, with an average annual salary of INR 5.8 lakhs for the students. Some students were even fortunate to receive an attractive stipend of INR 80,000 per month, clearly highlighting the university's dedication to providing its students with lucrative and rewarding employment prospects.
