BMS College of Engineering

About College

Bhusanayana Mukundadas Sreenivasaiah Institution of Engineering (BMSCE), commonly known as B.M.S. College of Engineering, is a renowned private engineering college located in Basavanagudi, Bangalore. Established by Bhusanayana Mukundadas Sreenivasaiah in 1946, the administration of the college falls under the B.M.S Educational Trust. BMS College of Engineering offers a wide range of programs at the undergraduate (UG), postgraduate (PG), and PhD levels. The flagship program is the Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.), and the institution also provides opportunities for PhD and MSc (Engineering by Research) programs. BMSCE has 14 departments designated as Research Centers. Admissions to all courses at BMSCE are granted based on various state and national-level entrance tests, such as PGCET, KMAT, MAT, GATE, and others. Some courses may involve additional counseling sessions for seat allocation. Students are required to register for a course twice – once on the official website of the entrance exam and next on the official website of BMSCE.

BMS College of Engineering has established collaborations with eight national and international institutions over the past decade. These Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs) are aimed at fostering industry and research training among students. The partnerships include institutions like National Aerospace Labs in Bangalore, Nopo Technologies in Bangalore, Gothenburg University in Sweden, SAII in the United States, NIPER in Kolkata, and Image Labels Pvt. Ltd., among others.

The college provides various scholarships to support students in need and those with exceptional academic performance. These include private scholarships, government scholarships, and the Huawei Scholarship for Excellence. Additionally, government scholarship programs offer numerous opportunities for financial assistance.

In terms of research, BMSCE has made significant contributions. The college’s 14 departments have submitted 61 patent applications and published numerous research papers. The institution has recognized 137 Ph.D. holders and 22 M.Sc graduates for their research achievements. Moreover, 329 research scholars from different departments are enrolled in research centers, working on projects within their respective fields of study.

The faculty at BMSCE is highly qualified, with approximately 70% of members holding a Ph.D. in their areas of interest-based research. They also possess 5 to 10 years of teaching experience, making them a highly skilled academic team.

Accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with highest grade of A++ in the second Cycle with a CGPA of 3.83 on a scale of four. Valid from 28.03.2019 to 28.03.2024.

Approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi



Admissions
The admission procedure at BMS College of Engineering kicks off in January, starting with registrations for MBA and MCA courses. For undergraduate programs, the application process typically begins in February. It's important to note that the application process is conducted offline. Candidates are required to download and print the application form from the college's official website, and then submit it before the specified deadline.

Admissions to undergraduate courses are based on COMEDK scores, while MBA and MCA admissions hinge on KMAT scores. For M.Tech. and M.Arch programs, admissions are determined by PGCET scores. There may be certain relaxations in academic eligibility criteria, primarily applicable to Karnataka candidates belonging to the Scheduled Caste, Scheduled Tribe, and Other Backward Classes. This demonstrates the college's commitment to inclusivity and providing opportunities to deserving candidates from various backgrounds.

Placements

As per media reports, over 1,000 offers were extended to around 1,300 eligible students during the placements at BMS College of Engineering in 2023. Previously, the placement rate for BTech students at BMS College of Engineering in 2022 was noted at 80%. According to the NIRF report for 2023 from BMS College of Engineering, the median package offered during BTech placements in 2022 was INR 8.24 LPA. Furthermore, data available on the official website indicates that more than 350 companies participate in the campus recruitment process at BMS College of Engineering on an annual basis. These figures highlight the strong placement opportunities available to students at the institution.
