BITS Pilani

About the College

Views: 0

BITS Pilani, situated in Pilani, Rajasthan, is a distinguished higher education and research university. It holds the prestigious ‘Institute of Eminence’ status, being one of the first six institutes to receive this recognition. The university operates across four campuses located in Pilani, Goa, Hyderabad, and Dubai. The Dubai campus makes BITS Pilani the first internationally deemed university. Comprising 15 academic departments in Science, Engineering, Management, and Research, BITS Pilani also houses nine diverse centers focusing on areas like Software Development, Robotics, Women Studies, and Desert Development Technologies.

With a student-faculty ratio of 7.9, BITS Pilani follows a three-tier academic structure. At the first level, it offers integrated first-degree programs in BE, BPharma, and MSc. The flagship BE program requires applicants to take the BITSAT exam, with an expected cutoff of around 270 marks for 2023. Notably, BITS Pilani extends direct admission to state board toppers in PCM and PCB streams for the BE and BPharma programs, respectively. The initial year fees for the flagship BE program amount to approximately 5.42 Lakhs. Higher degree programs like ME and MPharma are available, and admission is based on the BITS HD exam. Additionally, the university offers on-campus, off-campus, and part-time PhD programs. BITS Pilani is renowned for its Work Integrated Learning Programs (WILP) tailored for working professionals. WILP provides technical continuing education in areas like MTech, BTech, MBA, MSc, PG Diploma, and PG Certificate programs. These UGC-approved courses are recognized nationally and internationally

In terms of rankings, BITS Pilani has secured a position in the top 20 universities in India according to NIRF 2023 Rankings. In the NIRF 2023 Pharmacy Rankings, it stands among the top three colleges in India. The university has an outstanding placement track record, concluding the 2023 placement drive with a 31.5% increase in average salary and a 35% increase in the highest salary. The highest offered salary reached INR 60.75 LPA, while the average stood at INR 30.37 LPA. Notable recruiters include Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Visa, Adobe, and Cisco, among others.

BITS Pilani Admission Process 2024

BITS Pilani offers four Integrated First Degree courses across the streams of Science, Technology, Pharmacy, Management, and Humanities. These courses include BE, BPharm, MSc, and MSc (Tech). Apart from these, BITS Pilani also offers three higher degree courses, including ME, MPharm, and MBA. The duration of all these courses is two years. The Deemed University conducts the BITS Higher Degree Online Test (BITS HD Online Test) usually in the month of August for admission to ME and MPharm courses or accepts admission via GATE/ GPAT scores.
