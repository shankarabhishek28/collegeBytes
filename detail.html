SRM University

About the College

Views: 0

SRM Institute of Science and Technology, formerly known as the SRM University was established in 1985. SRM University has a total of 8 campuses, and one such campus is located in Kattankulathur, Chennai. SRM University Chennai is accredited by IET, NAAC, MHRD and ABET and is approved by the UGC. According to the NIRF Ranking 2023, SRM university has secured the 6th rank under the dental category.As per the NIRF Ranking 2022, SRM Institute of Science and Technology has bagged 36 th rank in the overall category, 19 in the university category, 35 in the research category, 24 in the engineering category, 12 in the pharmacy category, 20 in the medical category, 8 in the dental category, and secured 11th rank in the architecture category. SRM University Chennai campus is spread over 250+ acres of land comprising 42 blocks and provides education to 50,000+ students. Be it SRM University Chennai curriculum, SRMIST infrastructure, or facilities, all are directed towards the holistic development of the students. Some of the prominent SRM University Chennai facilities are classrooms, spacious labs, libraries, a knowledge centre, AC-equipped auditorium, prayer halls, high-speed wi-fi, bookstores, dining options, ATMs, cafeterias, gyms and more. Moreover, separate SRM University Chennai hostels are also available for female and male students.

SRM University Chennai courses are offered at graduate, postgraduate, diploma, PG diploma, and doctoral levels. SRM University Chennai has nine institutes and more than 111+ departments. There are a total of 300+ SRM University Chennai courses with a total of 3200+ academicians. SRM University Chennai admissions are offered to all its courses on the basis of entrance exams for some courses and on the basis of merit for others. SRM University Chennai accepts international admissions as well. For the record, SRM University Chennai accepts over 6000+ applicants every year for admissions to its courses. SRM University Chennai scholarships are also available and around Rs 30+ Cr are provided to the students every year under various scholarship schemes. As per the SRM University Chennai placement record for 2022, over 650+ companies visited the SRMIST campus placement drive and over 7030+ offers were made.

Accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with highest grade of A++ in the second Cycle with a CGPA of 3.55 on a scale of four.

Approved by All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi

Admission

SRM University Chennai Admission Process

To start with the SRM University Chennai admission process for engineering and technology courses, candidates need to register for the respective entrance exam, appear and qualify it. For those SRM University Chennai courses, where admissions are done on the basis of merit, candidates can directly start with the registration process.

Candidates need to register themselves by visiting the SRM University Chennai official website, fill up the application form and paying the application fee through SRM University Chennai e-payment system.

After the entrance exam is released, candidates will be shortlisted based on the SRM University Chennai cut off 2023 list. For SRM University Chennai admission for the non-entrance courses, candidates will be shortlisted based on their marks in the qualifying exam.

Shortlisted candidates will then attend the counselling session where they will be allotted seats as per the course chosen and availability of seats.

Candidates can now verify the documents and pay the other requisite fees to complete their admission process.

SRM University Chennai Application Process

Candidates need to mandatorily fill up the SRM University Chennai application form as a part of the admission process.

Candidates must keep in mind that all fields marked with ‘’ in the application form should be filled compulsorily.

Along with the self-attested scanned documents candidates should keep in handy - Photograph Image: Size should be between 10 kb and 200 kb with a dimension of 3.5 cm X 4.5 cm. Signature Image: Size should be between 10 kb and 200 kb with a dimension of 3.5 cm X 1.5 cm.

After duly filling up and uploading the documents, candidates need to pay the SRM University Chennai application fee and submit the application form.

After the successful submission of the SRM University application form, an application number will be generated. Candidates must save that number for future reference and communications during the admission process.

Courses
Course Eligibility Criteria
B.Tech
  • Resident Indian or Non-Resident Indian (NRI), holder of PIO or OCI card issued by Government of India
  • Should have attained the age of 16 years and 6 months on the 31st July of the calendar year in which the class 12th examination is to be held
  • Minimum 50% aggregate in PCM
  • Must have attempted Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, English & Aptitude in SRMJEEE(UG)
  • Direct Admission: SRM offers direct admission and scholarships to first rank students of all the central and state boards in India, top 10,000 rankers in IIT JEE, top rankers in each district of Tamil Nadu and exemplary sports persons at National and International level.
B.Des
  • Pass in 10+2 or any other equivalent examination of any authority recognised by SRMIST with a minimum of 50% aggregate/percentage of marks. The candidate can belong to any group of study (Science, Commerce, Fine Arts or any other stream).
  • Criteria 1: UCEED Score + (Written Exam + Personal Interview) at SRMIST
  • Criteria 2: Written Exam + Personal Interview at SRMIST
  • Written exam and personal interview will be conducted by the school of architecture and interior design, SRMIST. UCEED scores will be given more preference.
B.Arch
  • Candidate must pass 10+2 with at least 50% aggregate marks in physics, chemistry & mathematics or pass 10+3 diploma examination with mathematics as a compulsory subject with at least 50% marks in aggregate.
  • Pass in National Aptitude Test in Architecture (NATA) conducted by the Council of Architecture, IITJEE Mains – Paper II
M.Arch
  • B.Arch. or equivalent, written exam and personal interview will be conducted by the School Of Architecture and Interior Design, SRMIST
  • Criteria 1: CEED Score + (Written Exam + Personal Interview) at SRMIST
  • Criteria 2: Written Exam + Personal Interview at SRMIST
M.Des
  • B.Arch. or equivalent
  • Criteria 1: CEED Score + (Written Exam + Personal Interview) at SRMIST
  • Criteria 2: Written Exam + Personal Interview at SRMIST
PG Diploma in Advanced Life Science Technology
  • M.Sc or M.Tech in life sciences/M.V.Sc. PG final year students can also apply.
