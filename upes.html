University of Petroleum and Energy Studies

About the College

Views: 0

The University Of Petroleum And Energy Studies, commonly known as UPES Dehradun, was established under the UPES Act of 2003. It holds approval from the University Grants Commission (UGC) and is a proud member of the Association of Indian Universities (AIU). The institution has received accreditation from NAAC, earning a Grade ‘A’. UPES encompasses expansive campuses spanning 44 acres in Dehradun, known as UPES Bidholi and UPES Kandoli. In the NIRF 2023 rankings, UPES secured the 79th position in the overall category and the 52nd position among universities.

UPES offers over 20 specialized undergraduate and postgraduate programs that are closely aligned with industry requirements. These programs are administered through its seven distinct schools, namely the School of Engineering, School of Computer Science, School of Design, School of Law, School of Business, School of Health Sciences, and School of Modern Media. The flagship program, UPES B.Tech, offers 20 different specializations. Admission to the B.Tech program is contingent on performance in JEE Main or the UPESEAT conducted by the university. The total fee for the program amounts to INR 15.21 lakhs. The MBA program at UPES is also highly sought after, and admission is determined by performance in CAT/XAT/MAT/UPESMET. The course fee for UPES MBA stands at INR 14.20 lakhs. Additionally, UPES Dehradun provides distance learning opportunities through CCE-UPES Distance Learning Education.

The placements for the year 2023 at UPES have been successfully concluded, with over 2000 offers extended. The highest package reached an impressive INR 36 Lakh Per Annum (LPA), while the average package stood at INR 8.66 LPA. Prominent recruiters such as Amazon, ONGC, Boeing, and Microsoft India participated in the placement drive. In addition to this, UPES has forged collaborations with various organizations and international universities to enhance both industrial and academic activities. The institution also offers specialized programs in conjunction with Microsoft across disciplines like B.Tech, M.Tech, B.Sc, B.Pharm, MBA, B.Des, and M.Des.

Accredited by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) with highest grade of A++ in the second Cycle with a CGPA of 3.02 on a scale of four.

UPES is recognized by University Grants Commission (UGC) under Section 2(f) of UGC Act, 1956. The Degrees awarded by UPES are recognized by the UGC.

Admission

The University of Petroleum and Energy Studies (UPES) provides a comprehensive range of full-time undergraduate, postgraduate, and Ph.D. programs. Additionally, the university offers part-time Ph.D. programs for those seeking flexible learning options. UPES Admissions are bifurcated into two main categories: Examination Pathway and Non-Examination Pathway. Under the Examination Pathway, candidates are required to participate in Entrance Exams conducted by UPES. Conversely, for the Non-Examination Pathway, admission is granted based on Merit or Scores obtained in National Level Entrance Examinations. The university also facilitates education through Distance Learning for added accessibility.

Admission to B.Tech and the integrated B.Tech-LLB (Hons.) courses at UPES is contingent on performance in the UPESEAT, followed by central counseling. Alternatively, candidates can also apply based on a valid score in the JEE Main, falling under the non-examination category.

For M.Des and B.Des programs, admission is predicated on a valid score in CEED or UPES DAT, followed by a Presentation and Interview. BBA admission is conducted through UPESMET-UG as well as National Level Tests. The selection process for LLB courses depends on the scores achieved in ULSAT or CLAT / LSAT. Interested candidates can apply for these courses until July 31st, 2022.

UPES also offers an MBA program with various specializations. Selection for this course is determined through UPESMET or National Level Entrance Examinations, followed by Group Discussion and Personal Interview.

Placements

In 2023, the placement drive at UPES Dehradun concluded with an impressive tally of over 2000 offers extended to students. The highest package offered stood at an impressive INR 36 Lakh Per Annum (LPA), showcasing the remarkable opportunities available to UPES graduates. Additionally, the average package at UPES Dehradun reached a commendable INR 8.66 LPA, reflecting the institution's dedication to fostering career growth.

A diverse array of roles were offered during the UPES Dehradun Placements 2023, including positions like Business Analyst, Cyber Security Analyst, Consultants, Revenue Managers, Data Analyst, and Software Engineer, among others. This range of opportunities demonstrates the breadth of industries and sectors that actively seek out UPES graduates.

Some of the most prominent and respected names in the industry participated in the placement drive at UPES Dehradun. Notable recruiters included ONGC, Airbus, Boeing, Microsoft India, Zeta, Amazon, and Oracle. This signifies the strong industry connections that UPES has cultivated, resulting in substantial employment prospects for its students.
