Manipal University

About the College

Views: 0

MAHE, founded in 1993, holds the status of a deemed university. It provides an extensive array of over 300 undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across 31 disciplines. Additionally, MAHE Manipal extends lateral entry opportunities for seven undergraduate courses. At the research level, the institution offers both fellowships and doctoral programs. Admission into MAHE programs is determined by factors such as performance in the MAHE MET (Manipal entrance exam), national-level entrance exams, or academic merit, contingent on the specific program. In the 2023 QS World University Rankings, MAHE secures a position between 751 and 800, reaffirming its standing as a respected institution of higher learning. The university also commands a prominent presence in rankings by NIRF, The Week, and Outlook, among other authoritative agencies. According to the MAHE NIRF 2022 report, 142 out of 739 undergraduate students and 1,171 out of 1,667 postgraduate students successfully secured placements. The median packages offered stand at INR 10.8 LPA (for postgraduates) and INR 3 LPA (for undergraduates). MAHE actively engages in global academic collaborations, boasting more than 250 partnerships with esteemed institutions like the University of Birmingham, Boston University, and Trinity College Dublin. The university is equipped with world-class learning facilities that enhance the educational experience. Notably, it is home to Asia’s largest anatomy museum and the most extensive life sciences library. Moreover, Manipal houses its own business incubation center and various centers of excellence, further solidifying its reputation in academic circles. With campuses in Mangaluru and Bengaluru, as well as offshore establishments in Dubai (UAE) and Melaka (Malaysia), MAHE offers a diverse and dynamic learning environment for its students.

Admission

Established in 1953 in Manipal, Karnataka, Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE) is currently accepting admissions for its undergraduate and postgraduate programs for the year 2023. Selection of candidates is based on their performance in the qualifying examination. The undergraduate courses include B.Tech (Regular+Lateral), B.Pharm, BBA, PharmD, B.Arch, and BA (Media & Communication). At the postgraduate level, programs like M.Sc (Medical), M.Sc (Nursing), ME, M.Tech, M.Pharm, Pharm. D (PB), and other M.Sc courses are also available. Additionally, Manipal University offers M.Phil and PhD courses. Admissions for most programs are granted through the online Manipal Entrance Test (MET), followed by Group Discussion (GD) and Personal Interview (PI) conducted by MAHE. The MET 2023 Application Form is accessible online, with a fee of INR 600/- (INR 1400 for selected courses).

Moreover, the university also considers scores from national level entrance tests such as NATA/ JEE Paper-II for B.Arch, NEET PG/UG, NEET SS, and NEET MDS for MD/MBBS/BDS, MS, MDS, and DM/M.Ch courses respectively. For M.Phil & PhD admissions, candidates are evaluated based on the entrance test, past academic performance, and an interview. Candidates who have cleared UGC NET/ CSIR NET/ ICMR JRF/ JEST or hold an INSPIRE fellowship are exempted from MAHE's Ph.D. entrance test.


Placements

For the graduating batch of 2023, the placement process is currently in progress. As of now, an impressive 92.9% of students have already secured placements. The institute welcomed a total of 339 recruiters on campus, with the highest number of recruiters showing interest in BTech courses. The Manipal Institute of Technology achieved an average package of INR 12.59 LPA for the 2023 batch, with the highest package reaching an impressive INR 54.75 LPA. Additionally, the median package stood at INR 8.5 LPA. Some of the sought-after career profiles offered during the placements included roles in AI Engineering, HR Analytics, UI Development, and Associate Data Scientist.









Notably, there was a substantial increase of nearly 20% in the overall average package for the 2023 batch when compared to the preceding year. Moreover, the placement rate for 2023 witnessed a significant surge of almost 18% compared to the previous year. This indicates a robust and thriving placement scenario at MIT Manipal for the class of 2023.
